My Story

Hi! I'm Klein Socrates a marketer, brand strategist, and now a full-stack developer in training. After years of building brands and leading creative teams, I decided to take a bold leap: learn how to code and build the products I imagine.

I’m the co-founder of Jara AI, Africa’s first AI-powered branding and marketing platform designed for startups, SMEs, and creators who deserve world-class marketing without agency fees. Learning to code is my way of building better, faster, and without limits.

I’m documenting my journey publicly, every lesson, every struggle, every win and as I move from beginner to someone capable of shipping full production-ready apps. My mission? To create tools that empower African entrepreneurs and creators to grow without friction.

This portfolio is the starting point of that journey. In three months, I plan to be proficient enough to build web apps, AI-powered tools, and features for Jara AI.
